voluntary activity

Volunteering is a rewarding activity that allows me to help others and make a positive impact in the world. I have participated in various volunteering activities in different places, such as Mekedoiya , Gergesenon, and Abebechi Gubena Charity.In Mekedoiya, I helped to distribute food, water, and clothes to the people who are in that place. I also taught some basic literacy and numeracy skills to the children who did not have access to education. In Gergesenon, I assisted in building houses, schools, and clinics for the community that lacked proper infrastructure and facilities. I also helped to plant trees and crops to improve the environment and livelihood of the people. In Abebechi Gubena Charity, I supported the elderly and disabled people who were living in poverty and isolation. I provided them with companionship, care, and comfort. I also helped to organize recreational and social activities for them to enjoy.
